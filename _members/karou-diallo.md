## Biography

Karou Diallo received the Master's degree from the School of Control and Computer Engineering, North China Electric Power University, Beijing, China, in 2021. He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. at Polytechnique Montréal under the supervision of Prof. Amal Zouaq. His research interests include neural machine translation, question answering, large language models, knowledge bases, and the Semantic Web. Outside of research, he has a strong passion for astronomy documentaries, running and comedy.

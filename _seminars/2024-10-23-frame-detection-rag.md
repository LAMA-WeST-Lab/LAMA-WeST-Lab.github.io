## Enhancing Frame Detection with Generative Models

*Speaker: Karou*

**Topics:** Enhancing Frame Detection with Generative Classifier in RAG Systems

### Abstract

In this seminar, we will discuss RAG-based generative approaches for frame detection and explore how structured representations of natural language questions impact knowledge graph question answering. We will also reflect on the use of non-parametric memories with LLMs.
